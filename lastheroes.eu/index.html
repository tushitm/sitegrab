Welcome to Last Heroes

The world of Last Heroes is a wasteland. The ARC empire destroyed it with a single Mutagen Bomb that transformed it’s creatures to mutated monsters. Become the Gunsword heroes in this classic adventure and survival as they become the last hope for the United Resistance. Gain powerful abilities to dash, spin, and blast through creepy creatures, mutated soldiers and huge bosses. It’s up to you to stop the ARC from achieving world domination.


Features (beta):

- A rank system to have rewards and be in the top of the world!
- Daily missions with rewards every day!
- Several characters with different levels, different items and different skills!
- Receive messages and gifts in real time from your friends!
+15 world and level available for beta, test the game now!
And more in the future !
